5. How to Request a Free Legal Consultation? (Step-by-Step)

If you require a detailed review of land records or mutation forms, please follow these ordered steps:

  1. Collect Documents: Gather all land papers, registered deeds, or death certificates related to the case.
  2. Compose Request: Draft a summary describing the dates, location, and nature of the land dispute.
  3. Submit Case: Send the details to advocate-panel@gramin-nyaya-society.org or request support via the main kiosk terminal.
  4. Receive Call: A verified panel lawyer will call you back within 24 working hours to guide you.

8. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common support and contact queries below:

How long does it take to get a reply to my email?

Typically, general support replies within 24-48 hours. Legal aid panel reviews may take up to 3 working days depending on lawyer availability.

Can I visit the DLSA head office without an appointment?

Yes, walk-ins are welcome from 10 AM to 1 PM, Monday to Friday. However, booking an slot via email is recommended for advice.
